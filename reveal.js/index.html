Getting a lower bound for the worst case performance $\rho$ of TilePacking. \begin{equation} \rho \geq 0.09121 \end{equation} Looking at the set of rectangles $R = \{r_i:i=1,\ldots,n\}$ from a TilePacking, if \begin{equation*} \text{area}(r_i) \geq 0.09121 * \text{area}(t_i) \end{equation*} for every $i$, we would be done.
